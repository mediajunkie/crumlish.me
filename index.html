Christian Crumlish

My name is Christian, but you can spell it Xian (or even xian, which looks better to my eye). I’m a product person, team leader & music lover.

I consult on Product & UX leadership at Design in Product.

Most recently, I've been working on COVID19.CA.GOV and cannabis.ca.gov for the state of California's office of innovation. When the pandemic first hit, I joined a volunteer effort spearheaded by an MIT Media Lab professor and built a product team at the PathCheck Foundation. I was VP of Product at 7 Cups, Senior Director of Product & UX at CloudOn (acquired by Dropbox in 2015), Director of Product for AIM, & the last curator of the Yahoo design pattern library.

Ukulele

When I’m not consulting, writing, or speaking, I’m either playing or writing songs on my ukulele.
